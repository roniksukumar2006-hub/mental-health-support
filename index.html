Frequently Asked Questions

What services are available for mental health support?

▼

We offer various services including counseling, crisis support, self-help resources, and referrals to mental health professionals.

How can I schedule a counseling session?

▼

You can schedule a counseling session through our chat interface or by contacting the student support services directly.

Is the service confidential?

▼

Yes, all conversations and services are completely confidential and follow strict privacy guidelines.

What should I do in a crisis situation?

▼

In case of an emergency, contact emergency services immediately. For urgent support, use our 24/7 crisis helpline.
